The release was softer than forecasts and pulled the immediate tightening impulse out of rates, the dollar and gold. [BLS](https://www.bls.gov/news.release/cpi.nr0.htm) · [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/us-consumer-inflation-slows-more-than-expected-june-2026-07-14/)

Chair Kevin Warsh still refused to declare victory on inflation, so this is a market repricing, not a central-bank pivot. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/feds-warsh-heads-congress-after-tentative-steps-away-trump-2026-07-14/)
- **The energy shock did not follow CPI lower.** Brent settled at $84.73 and WTI at $79.34; European gas rose above its trigger as impaired Gulf traffic preserved the physical premium. That is why one soft inflation print does not yet break the regime. [Reuters markets](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/global-markets-global-markets-2026-07-14/) · [EIA](https://www.eia.gov/outlooks/steo/)
- **Gold passed the first dovish-reaction test, not the full override test.** Spot gold recovered toward $4,052 as the 10-year real yield fell to 2.33%; silver, platinum and palladium also rose. Gold still needs either $4,150 or repeated strength alongside firm yields/DXY to overturn the active sign-flip. [Reuters gold](https://www.reuters.com/world/india/gold-recovers-two-week-low-ahead-us-inflation-figures-2026-07-14/) · [US Treasury real yields](https://home.treasury.gov/resource-center/data-chart-center/interest-rates/TextView?type=daily_treasury_real_yield_curve&field_tdr_date_value=2026)
- **Risk assets accepted the CPI relief selectively.** The S&P 500 rose 0.38% and Nasdaq 0.90%, while the ASX finished near flat as energy gains offset bank weakness. Copper and iron ore strengthened ahead of China’s GDP and activity release, which now has to validate the industrial move. [Reuters markets](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/global-markets-global-markets-2026-07-14/) · [China preview](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/chinas-q2-growth-set-lose-steam-stimulus-remain-calibrated-2026-07-14/)
