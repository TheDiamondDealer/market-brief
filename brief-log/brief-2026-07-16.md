## Regime verdict

No live regime file amendment is warranted today. One formal de-escalation condition—September hike pricing below 50%—has occurred, and rates/DXY have weakened for two sessions. But Brent is near $85, Gulf flows remain impaired, DXY is not below 100, the US 10-year is not below 4.45%, and gold is not above $4,150. The standing regime is therefore **intact but under pressure**, with the policy leg weakened and energy still dominant.
