# Operating Model — Three Altitudes

This system is a cascade, not three independent reports.

## Daily — Tactical

Schedule: weekdays at 8:30 AM Australia/Melbourne.

Purpose:

- Explain what moved overnight and why.
- Apply the approved regime and active sign-flips.
- Lead with breached thresholds.
- Identify today's and this week's immediate catalysts.
- Avoid re-deriving durable market structure.
- Convert the completed research into the dashboard news feed and political trackers.

Reads:

- Approved baseline dossier.
- Live regime state.
- Live thresholds.
- Three most recent daily logs.
- `prompts/site-update.md` for dashboard data rules.

Writes:

- One dated daily brief.
- A dated regime note only when there is clear evidence of a material shift.
- An updated `site/intelligence-data.js` containing the interpreted news feed, Trump policy/disclosure tracker and Nancy Pelosi disclosure tracker.

## Weekly — Operational

Schedule: Sunday at 9:00 AM Australia/Melbourne.

Sunday is preferred to Saturday because Friday's US close is fully digested and the report can provide a complete week-ahead runway.

Purpose:

- Synthesize the closed week rather than join five daily reports together.
- Calculate or verify net weekly moves.
- Identify the two or three themes that mattered.
- Test whether the regime held.
- Build the week-ahead catalyst calendar.
- Separate signal from noise.

Reads:

- Approved baseline dossier.
- Live regime state and thresholds.
- All daily logs from the completed week.

Writes:

- One dated weekly review.
- A dated regime amendment only when supported by material evidence.

## Monthly — Strategic

Schedule: first Melbourne business day of each month at 9:00 AM Australia/Melbourne.

Purpose:

- Regenerate the strategic frame using deep current research.
- Reassess durable market structure and the current state of play.
- Formally classify the regime as intact, shifting or broken.
- Reset headline trigger thresholds.
- Set the one-to-three-month outlook and swing factors.

Reads:

- Live approved dossier, regime and thresholds.
- Last four or five weekly reports.

Writes:

- Draft dossier.
- Draft regime state.
- Draft thresholds.
- Strategic monthly review.

## Approval boundary

The monthly scheduled task must never overwrite the live baseline dossier, regime state or thresholds.

Promotion requires an explicit user instruction identifying the month and stating approval. The approval task must show a concise diff summary before replacing live files. If approval is ambiguous, it must stop and ask.
