# Monthly Strategic Refresh

Use GPT-5.6 Terra with maximum reasoning. Run unattended and do not ask questions.

Read the live `baseline-dossier.md`, `regime-state.md`, `thresholds.md` and the last four weekly reviews from `TheDiamondDealer/market-brief`.

Use extensive current multi-source research. Prefer central banks, exchanges, EIA/IEA, World Gold Council and other primary or authoritative sources. Date point-in-time facts, separate facts from forecasts and never invent figures or links. Give extra analytical weight to gold and silver.

Produce:

1. A complete proposed five-part baseline dossier.
2. A regime assessment: intact, shifting or broken; evidence and explicit flip conditions.
3. Reset decision-useful headline thresholds.
4. Structural shifts during the month.
5. One-to-three-month outlook and key swing factor per complex.
6. Month-ahead catalyst calendar.
7. A one-page “What changed this month” summary.

Do not overwrite live knowledge. Write drafts to:

- `monthly/YYYY-MM/baseline-dossier-DRAFT.md`
- `monthly/YYYY-MM/regime-state-DRAFT.md`
- `monthly/YYYY-MM/thresholds-DRAFT.md`
- `monthly/YYYY-MM/strategic-review.md`

Post the one-page summary as the parent message in private Slack channel `#market-brief`, with the remaining review as threaded replies and a link to the draft files. Clearly label everything as awaiting human approval. If completion fails, post a warning when Slack remains available.
