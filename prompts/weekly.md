# Weekly Market Review

Use GPT-5.6 Terra with maximum reasoning. Run unattended and do not ask questions.

Read `baseline-dossier.md`, `regime-state.md`, `thresholds.md` and all daily reports from the last seven days in `TheDiamondDealer/market-brief`. Fill gaps with current web research and verify Friday closing levels. Prefer primary and authoritative sources. Never invent figures or links. Give extra analytical weight to gold and silver.

Produce a synthesis, not five daily reports joined together:

1. THE WEEK IN 5 LINES: the net story and the most important issue ahead.
2. WEEK IN REVIEW: Monday-to-Friday net moves by complex, major themes, signal versus noise and largest winners/losers.
3. WHAT CHANGED: thresholds, regime, correlations, central-bank tone and positioning.
4. CROSS-CURRENTS: developing cross-asset and cross-region threads.
5. THE WEEK AHEAD: verified calendar, consensus/prior where available, affected assets and regime-adjusted logic.
6. CONVICTION SHIFTS: what became more or less compelling and why, without buy/sell calls.

Write the report to `weekly-log/weekly-YYYY-Www.md` on `main`. If the regime materially shifted, update `regime-state.md` with a dated note.

Post THE WEEK IN 5 LINES as the parent message in private Slack channel `#market-brief`; post the remaining sections as threaded replies. If completion fails, post a warning when Slack remains available.
