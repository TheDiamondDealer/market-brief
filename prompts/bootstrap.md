# Bootstrap Task

Use GPT-5.6 Terra with maximum reasoning. Run unattended and do not ask questions.

Use extensive current web research and prioritise primary or authoritative sources. Confirm dates and exact URLs. Never invent prices, statistics, consensus values, sources or links.

Build the initial five-part baseline dossier described in `baseline-dossier.md`. Cover the entire stated scope, giving extra analytical weight to gold and silver. Clearly distinguish durable mechanisms from the dated state of play.

Also create:

- A current `regime-state.md` that explicitly says whether the carry-over hawkish regime is intact, shifting or broken, with evidence, active sign-flips and clear flip conditions.
- Current decision-useful `thresholds.md` with headline triggers and risk zones.

For every risk zone include:

- asset or relationship
- warning band or point boundary
- headline trigger
- confirmation condition
- invalidation or exit condition
- expected direction under the proposed regime
- source and as-of date

Avoid false precision. If evidence supports only a single threshold, use a point and label it as such. Include at least gold, Brent, ASX 200, policy/hike odds and USD/JPY.

The dossier's State of Play must also include an initial relationship scorecard for:

- gold versus real yields and DXY
- oil versus inflation and policy expectations
- yen carry health versus global risk
- China demand versus iron ore, copper, AUD and ASX materials

Classify each relationship as confirmed, diverging or inconclusive and explain the evidence.

Write all three completed files directly to the `main` branch of `TheDiamondDealer/market-brief`.

Then post a concise message to the private Slack channel `#market-brief` stating:

- the bootstrap completed
- the current regime verdict
- the relationship scorecard
- the proposed headline thresholds and risk zones
- a link to the GitHub repository

If completion fails, post a warning to `#market-brief` with the failure reason if Slack remains available.
